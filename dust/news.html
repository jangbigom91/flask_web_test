Busan City provides real-time information on fine dust by neighborhood

Busan City, In the future, it will be able to check the fine dust situation in the neighborhood in real time. The Busan Institute of Health and Environment announced on the 29th that it will launch a "real-time fine dust information by neighborhood" service in July to help citizens check air quality information by neighborhood and prepare for high concentrations of air pollution. Busan is the first among local governments across the country to provide real-time fine dust services.

"All-out efforts" to reduce fine dust at ports and airports in Incheon.

The Incheon Metropolitan Government plans to install CCTVs at six access roads to monitor old vehicles entering ports and airports this year, and conduct joint inspections of scattering dust generation sites in the port to encourage the installation of scattering dust suppression facilities.

Ulsan Institute of Health and Environment establishes 'fine dust research department'

In order to strengthen the responsiveness of fine dust in Ulsan, the "fine dust research department" will be established at the Ulsan Institute of Health and Environment, and the microbial department will be renamed as the food poisoning inspection department.

Sweden's 5% dementia is due to ultrafine dust.

Amid reports in countries around the world that ultrafine dust (PM2.5) pollution is related to the occurrence of dementia patients, a study found that 5% of dementia patients in Sweden are estimated to be caused by ultrafine dust (PM2.5).

Fine dust information weather service in partnership with Naver and KT

The fine dust data provided by Naver's weather used some 200 national network meters across the country, but added super-large air quality data collected by 2,200 KT meters across the country. KT's newly added fine dust meter is installed near facilities for the vulnerable to fine dust, such as base stations at breathing heights and schools and hospitals across the country with a large floating population, to measure the air quality felt by actual users.
